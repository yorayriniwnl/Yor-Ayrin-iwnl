# Execution Plan — Convert portfolio into opportunities

Objective: apply to roles, share the portfolio, attend interviews, and iterate on feedback until offers follow.

Weekly targets (suggested)
- Apply: 8–12 tailored applications per week.
- Outreach: 10 targeted LinkedIn messages / recruiter emails per week.
- Interviews: aim for 2–4 screens per week.

Day-to-day checklist
1. Identify roles: run saved searches on LinkedIn, Indeed, and targeted company pages; add matches to the tracker.
2. Tailor application: pick one top sentence from the role and update the cover/opening line to match (use the outreach templates).
3. Submit: use ATS or direct email; copy portfolio link and resume (use `QuickApply` section on site).
4. Track: update `data/application-tracker.csv` with status and next action.
5. Follow-up: 5–7 days after apply, send a concise follow-up via the template.

Share portfolio (daily/weekly)
- Post one short LinkedIn post highlighting Yor Zenith or a key result (use OG image + screenshot).
- DM 3–5 targeted contacts (hiring managers, ex-colleagues, recruiters) with the short outreach template.

Interview preparation
- Use `/delivery` recorder for short practice runs (30–90s answers).
- Run the demo flow and Yor Zenith script; record 2–3 takes and pick the best.
- Log feedback in the tracker after each interview.

Iterate and prioritize
- After each rejection or feedback, log reason (if provided) and rate impact 1–3.
- Prioritize fixes that increase clarity or fix common objections: (1) Hero copy, (2) Yor Zenith metrics, (3) Demo clarity, (4) Resume bullets.
- Make small changes, re-record, and re-share; track results (responses/interviews won).

Assets & references
- Outreach templates: [content/outreach-templates.md](content/outreach-templates.md)
- Application tracker CSV: [data/application-tracker.csv](data/application-tracker.csv)
- Demo flow checklist: [content/demo-flow.md](content/demo-flow.md)

Notes
- Keep all messages concise; lead with BLUF (bottom line up front).
- Use the mindset rule: "I am not trying to impress. I am showing how I think and build." Breathe and be concise.
