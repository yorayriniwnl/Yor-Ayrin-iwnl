# Yor Zenith - Solar Energy Planning & Decision Support System

1. What it is
Yor Zenith is a solar planning platform for rooftop feasibility analysis, solar energy generation estimation, and modular dashboard-based planning.

2. Why you built it
Users need a clearer way to judge rooftop solar viability before committing to installation decisions. Yor Zenith brings feasibility, generation forecasting, and subsidy-aware analysis into one project.

3. How it works
The project uses React and Three.js for interactive 3D visualization dashboards. It evaluates rooftop parameters, solar irradiance assumptions, and subsidy estimates, then presents solar potential and energy generation forecasts through modular UI panels.

4. What you learned
Solar planning is not just a calculation problem. It needs interface clarity, visual feedback, and careful explanation so technical and financial signals become understandable decisions.
