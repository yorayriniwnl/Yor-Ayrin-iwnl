# Demo Flow — Portfolio Walkthrough (2–3 minutes)

Purpose: a tight, recorded walkthrough that highlights your hero, deep-dives into Yor Zenith, briefly shows other projects, and closes with skills and CTA.

Total target: 2–3 minutes (optimal ~2:30).

Sequence & timing
- 0:00–0:15 — Hero (15s)
- 0:15–1:30 — Yor Zenith (75s)
- 1:30–2:00 — Other projects (30s)
- 2:00–2:20 — Skills (20s)
- 2:20–2:30 — Close / CTA (10s)

Detailed script & cues

1) Hero (0:00–0:15)
- Visual: show the top of the site (Hero + SummaryCard). Keep cursor still.
- Say (12–15s): "Hi, I'm [Your Name]. I build product systems that [one-line value statement]. Today I'll walk through Yor Zenith, a flagship project, then a few other highlights and my core skills. Resume and contact are available in the top-right."

Cue: pause 1–2s before clicking Projects or navigating to Yor Zenith.

2) Yor Zenith (0:15–1:30) — main focus
- Visual: open the Yor Zenith case study page. Show hero screenshot, architecture / key screenshot, and the results section (or summary card).
- Structure (STAR-style, approx times below):
  - Problem (10s): "The challenge: [one-sentence problem statement]."
  - Role & Approach (25–30s): "My role: [lead/engineer/designer]. I solved it by [approach — architecture, prioritized MVP, automation, measurements]." Show a diagram or code screenshot if helpful.
  - Outcome / Impact (20–25s): "Outcome: [brief impact statement — e.g., faster, more reliable, reduced cost/latency]." If you have a metric, state it confidently here.
  - Key takeaway (5–10s): one concise sentence linking the work to business value.

Tips for Yor Zenith section:
- Point only at elements you mention. Use slow mouse gestures and minimal scrolling.
- Narrate while showing the most credible artifact (screenshot, demo GIF, or metric panel).

3) Other projects (1:30–2:00)
- Visual: return to Projects list. For each project (2–3), show the title and one screenshot.
- Script (10–12s per project): "ProjectName — one-line problem and impact." Keep each to one sentence.

4) Skills (2:00–2:20)
- Visual: scroll to Skills section or show a simple skill list overlay.
- Script (20s total): "Core skills: systems thinking, ownership, and clear cross-team communication. For Yor Zenith, I led the architecture and delivery and paired closely with design and QA to ship safely."

5) Close / CTA (2:20–2:30)
- Visual: show Contact / QuickApply area or Navbar resume link.
- Script (8–10s): "If you'd like details or a walkthrough of a specific part, my resume and contact are linked. Thank you — I look forward to connecting."

Recording & delivery tips
- Record resolution: 1080p, mic only, use Do Not Disturb. Close unrelated tabs/apps.
- Count-in: use a 3s count-in. Pause 1–2s between points and sections.
- Pacing: speak slowly, avoid filler words, and keep sentences short (~10–15 words each).
- Mouse: move the cursor deliberately and avoid rapid tracking; use a highlight tool if available.
- Takes: record 2–3 takes and pick the best one; use the `/delivery` recorder to review and check filler words.
