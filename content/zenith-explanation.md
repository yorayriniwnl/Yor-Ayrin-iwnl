# Yor Zenith - Structured Explanation

1. Problem:
Rooftop solar planning requires users to reason about feasibility, expected generation, rooftop conditions, irradiance, and subsidy estimates before they can make a confident decision.

2. Solution:
I built a solar planning platform that analyzes rooftop feasibility and estimates solar energy generation through modular dashboards and interactive 3D visualization.

3. Tech:
React and Three.js for interactive visualization dashboards; Python for solar planning logic and analysis workflows; TypeScript for structured frontend development.

4. Outcome:
Helps turn solar planning signals into a clearer decision-support workflow for feasibility checks and energy generation forecasting.
